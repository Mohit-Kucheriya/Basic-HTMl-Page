  • Developement and Implementation of an Integrted web based document management system

    • Management System is an efficient, time saving and easy way to report, view and control the version of a file..As a good document management system it has a possibility to upload, download and delete files.These tools are often cloud-based. This means people can access the files they need anywhere with an internet connection. Document management system examples include Microsoft SharePoint, Amazon WorkDocs, and Dokkio.There are four main types of DMS: server-based, cloud-based, web-based and database. The most popular is cloud-based mainly due to its availability and user-friendly interface, however big companies tend to use server-based DMS as it is more secure and customizable.
